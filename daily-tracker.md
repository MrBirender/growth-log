# Daily Tracker

> Fill this in at the end of each day. One line per block. Honest answers only.
> Weekly review every Sunday. Monthly review on the last Sunday of each month.

---

## How to mark

- `✓` — done, full 30 min
- `~` — partial, less than 30 min but something
- `✗` — skipped
- Add one sentence about what you did or why you skipped. No judgment.
