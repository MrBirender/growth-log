# Daily Schedule

> Working hours: 9:30 AM – 6:00 PM (8.5 hrs)
> Break: 2 hours total (lunch + rest + tv — all in one block)
> Learning: 2.5 hours (5 × 30 min)
> Company work: 4 hours

---

## Time Blocks

| Time | Block | What happens |
|---|---|---|
| 9:30 – 10:00 | **DSA** | One problem or one concept. Agenda prepared by Claude. |
| 10:00 – 10:30 | **System Design** | One topic, one question, grounded in Bandyl. |

| 3:00 – 3:30 | **English** | Writing or speaking practice. One exercise. |
| 3:30 – 4:00 | **Flute** | Practice. No screens during this block. |
| 4:00 – 6:00 | **Company work** | Deep work block 2. |

---

## Rules

1. **Start on time, not when you feel like it.** The feeling comes after starting, not before.
2. **30 minutes means 30 minutes.** Set a timer. Stop when it ends. This keeps it sustainable.
3. **Never skip two days in a row.** One missed day is normal. Two is a pattern breaking.
4. **If a block feels impossible, do 10 minutes.** 10 > 0. Always.
5. **Check the daily tracker first thing.** It tells you exactly what to do — no deciding needed.
