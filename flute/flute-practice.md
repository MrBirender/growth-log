## How to use 30 minutes

A structured 30-minute session:

| Minutes | What to do |
|---|---|
| 0–5 | Long tones — play Sa and hold it as long as possible, focus on steady air |
| 5–20 | Sargam ascending and descending — slow, one note at a time, stop on any breaking note and repeat it 5 times |
| 20–28 | Play sargam at a comfortable pace without stopping — accept small breaks, keep going |
| 28–30 | Note which note broke most today (one line in the log) |
